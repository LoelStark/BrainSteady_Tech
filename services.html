Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about prices, timelines, or lessons? Here are quick answers to our most common inquiries.

We specialize in building fully static, lightweight, and modern websites using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Bootstrap. Static frontends are fast, extremely secure, and can be hosted completely free on platforms like GitHub Pages, meaning zero monthly database or server hosting fees for our clients.

All tutoring sessions are held remotely over platforms like Discord, Zoom, or Google Meet. We review your assignments or learning objectives in advance, share screens to write and debug code together, and explain underlying theories using practical examples.

Yes, we help students navigate programming homework, labs, and projects. However, we do not simply write code for you to copy. We act as mentors, guiding your logic, highlighting syntax errors, explaining memory allocation, and ensuring you can confidently explain your code in class.

A simple single-page portfolio or landing page generally takes 3 to 5 business days. More robust multi-page websites with custom layouts, forms, and galleries typically take 1 to 2 weeks, depending on content availability and revisions.

We supply all graphic assets in web-optimized formats (high-res PNG, JPG) as well as print-ready, high-resolution PDFs. Source files in vector format (SVG, SVG-supported PDFs) are also included in the branding package so you can scale them without losing quality.

For development and graphic design projects, we require a 50% deposit before work begins, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion and testing. For recurring tutoring sessions, lessons are billed on an hourly basis and invoiced at the end of each session or pre-purchased in weekly block bundles.
