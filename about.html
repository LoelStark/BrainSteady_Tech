WHO WE ARE

Our Story & Mission

Bridging the gap between software development and tech education, built from the ground up.

The Founder's Journey

Brainsteady Tech began as a solo freelance operation by a young developer passionate about making coding accessible and crafting efficient web spaces. Frustrated by dry, academic textbooks, he started a WhatsApp group in matric where he was offering tech advice to learners who wanted to break into tech at a young age.

We represent the new wave of developers: cloud-capable, security-focused, and committed to sharing knowledge. From designing pixel-perfect websites to configuring OpenStack virtual labs, our mission is rooted in education and practical experience.

"We believe that real coding experience combined with simple, visual mentoring is the absolute fastest way to master software engineering."

Our Mission

To democratize technical education while helping small businesses create stable, impressive digital presences.

Our Vision

To grow into a leading developer platform known for practical labs, premium software creations, and student mentorship.

Timeline

How Brainsteady Tech grew from an independent coding initiative into a multidisciplinary startup brand.

Q1 2024
The Genesis

Started as a WhatsApp group in matric where he was offering tech advice to learners that wanted to break into tech at a young age. The founder recognized the need for accessible, practical tech education and began mentoring peers passionate about coding.

Q3 2025
Expanding the Palette

Graphic design and static website creation services were established. He started creating basic websites and poster designs for students running local businesses and attending tech events to gain insights on how this works in the real world.

Q1 2025
Blossoming into a Tutoring Service

It then blossomed in his first year when BrainSteady became a tutoring service, giving free Python and basic ICT lessons to Engineering students at the Vaal University of Technology. From there he expanded and made it a WhatsApp channel with 200 members, posting job opportunities for recent graduates while starting his journey in ethical hacking, malware analysis and C++. This gave him the platform to create projects while teaching others how to start.

Q3 2025
Lab Environments & Professional Growth

Cloud-based labs (OpenStack virtualization networks) are deployed, allowing students to study security operations and infrastructure administration in real-time. Meanwhile, graphic design and website development services continue to flourish, providing students with professional digital portfolios and helping small businesses establish their online presence.

Q1 2026
Founded the Electrical Engineering Society

Founded the Electrical Engineering Society which propelled students on how to create dynamic websites for themselves while giving them access to IoT devices and CyberSecurity basics. This initiative expanded Brainsteady Tech's reach, enabling students to bridge engineering and software development while building practical, real-world skills.
